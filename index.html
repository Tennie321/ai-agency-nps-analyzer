Frequently Asked Questions

What is NPS and why does it matter?
Net Promoter Score (NPS) measures customer loyalty on a 0–10 scale. It's the single most predictive metric for retention and growth. Our tool automates scoring, benchmarking, and action planning so you can improve NPS without manual spreadsheet work.
What format do my survey responses need to be in?
The tool accepts CSV and JSON files. Export your survey data from Typeform, SurveyMonkey, Google Forms, or any platform that supports CSV/JSON export. The CLI auto-detects the score column and open-ended response fields.
Do I need Python or technical skills to use this?
Basic terminal familiarity helps, but the CLI is designed to be beginner-friendly: `nps-analyze your-file.csv` is all you need. Python 3.8+ is required, and there are zero external dependencies — no pandas, numpy, or ML frameworks to install.
How does the F.E.E.D.B.A.C.K. framework work?
F.E.E.D.B.A.C.K. stands for Find, Evaluate, Extract, Diagnose, Benchmark, Analyze, Conceptualize, and Keep improving. It's an 8-step methodology that guides you from raw survey data to a concrete retention improvement plan, baked directly into the tool's output.
